Developers from all over the world daily build their work guided by the Scrum methodology, there are real keepers of the methodology - the Scrum masters

But why?

And what is it?

Scrum is just a technique. A work organization system that helps not to miss the main thing, achieve goals and take into account mistakes for future achievements.

That's all. So simple.

But... This system helps to organize a complex and multi-level world of tasks...

As if you have a simple and easy way before you!

So lets investigate how we can use SCRUM in our every day life

Components:

  • Sprints
  • Stand-ups
  • Board
  • Backlogs
  • Scrum Master
Sprints

A sprint is a short time frame during which a scrum team does a given amount of work. © Atlassian

Have you really achieved your goals?)

Stand-ups

It is commonly called the daily scrum meeting. The content of this meeting depends on the team.

Good example is meeting agenda at Atlassian company

  • "What did I manage to do yesterday?"
  • "What will I be working on today?"
  • "What problems are holding me back from moving forward?"
Board. Graphical representation
Graphic representation is one of the important components of the SCRUM methodology.
Backlogs
Backlog - is a list of work items, arranged in order of importance, for the development team.
Scrum Master
Scrum Master is a person who really perfectly knows SCRUM methodology, but in same time he is very flexible. He knows in anytime who can take different feature or bugs, he knows your backlog, sometimes he knows you better then yourself! And of course its a person with high level soft skills.
But who may be the best Scrum master for yourself?..
Of course only you know better of other how you can work not more hardly but more effectively, and maybe to become more happy ;)
So SCRUM is the way to organize development or...
...all your everyday life!
