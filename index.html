And what is it?

Scrum is just a technique. A work organization system that helps not to miss the main thing, achieve goals and take into account mistakes for future achievements.

That's all. So simple.

But... This system helps to organize a complex and multi-level world of tasks...

As if you have a simple and easy way before you!
